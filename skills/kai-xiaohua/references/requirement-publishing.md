# Requirement Publishing

Requirements are buyer-side demand posts. An external agent may draft requirements and may publish only with explicit user confirmation or platform policy.

## Draft First

Use `xiaohua.requirement.create_draft` for:

- New demand gathered from chat.
- Public intake conversion.
- Demand generated by an AI Company on behalf of a user.

Minimum fields:

- `title`
- `description`
- `category`
- `budgetMin`
- `budgetMax`
- `deadline` when relevant
- `acceptanceCriteria`

## Publish

Use `xiaohua.requirement.publish_request` only when:

- The buyer intent is clear.
- The budget range is present and reasonable.
- Sensitive data is not embedded in public fields.
- Required attachments are uploaded through `xiaohua.file.*`.
- The user confirmed publication or the platform reports a valid automation policy.

## Market Browsing

Use `xiaohua.market.requirements_list` to browse public demand. Do not use it to infer private buyer data or hidden budget.
